Rules

Rules and regulations

* Only registered participants will be able to join the Workshop series as well as final Hackathon.

* A weekly task will be given for the participants to be submitted within 2-3 days after each workshop (further details will be given during each workshop).Those tasks will be given through the content in each workshop.

* It is mandatory to complete all the weekly tasks to participate for the Hackathon.

* Weekly tasks are scored and top scores will be displayed in a leaderboard.

* Hackathon will be conducted as an individual coding challenge for 4 hours hosting through HackerRank.

* Participants will be given assistance to familiarize with HackerRank via the workshop series.

* Registrations for the Hackathon will be announced after the completion of the workshop series.Registerd participants who have sit for A/L s at 2020 will be eligible for the Hackathon.

* All the participants who complete weekly tasks and competes at the Hackathon will be given valuable certificates and the Top 10 rankers will be awarded valuable prizes.

* 25% from the total scores for all weekly tasks will be added to scores earned at the Hackathon.

* The 4 requirements that should be fulfilled to participate the Hackathon.

  • should be a student who sat for A/L s at 2020
  • Should register for the Hackathon
  • Should have an attendance of 80% or above for the workshop serie(should participate minimum 4 workshops)
  • Should complete all the weekly tasks

* Participants who have participated minimum 4 workshops or earns 80% attendance or above for the workshop series(including introductory session+4 python sessions) and completes all the weekly will only be eligible for the Hackathon.

Terms & conditions for the competitors

> Every competitor should join the zoom session before the Hackathon and should confirm their prescence.Further instructions and guidelines will be provided at there.

> Competitors need to make sure they are competing their own and if any dishonesty or cheat found they will be disqualified.

> The decision of the Judgeboard will be the final.

> Any responsibility could not be taken by the Judgeboard or OC over hardware failures,power failures and connection losses happen during the competition.
